Welcome

Thanks for checking out the site!

I should probably set the expectations right from the start: this blog will not be regularly updated. In fact, I will be genuinely shocked if I stick with this for more than a month or two. But in the off-chance that I do, here is what you might expect to find:

  • side-projects spun off from my work as a data analyst with Futurewise
  • my thoughts on a variety of topics related to the focus of my studies and career: urban planning

My purpose is pretty simple: record my ideas somewhere where I can find them later, and if anyone else finds value in them that would be a nice bonus.

Stay tuned for fresh posts in the coming weeks.
